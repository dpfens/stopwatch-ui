Professional Timing Made Simple

You want to time multiple things at once without losing track of what belongs to who, or missing what's actually happening because you're fighting with your timing tool.

Most stopwatches are built for the simplest case: one timer, start/stop/reset. The native stopwatch on your smartphone works fine for timing a single thing. But real timing is messier.

02

You're tracking different people, recording various types of splits, dealing with things happening simultaneously. The gap between basic phone stopwatches and expensive enterprise timing systems leaves most people stuck with tools that don't fit how timing actually works.

This is a stopwatch app designed for that reality. It runs multiple independent timers, works offline, keeps your data local, and gets out of your way so you can focus on what's happening instead of wrestling with your timing tool.

03

What it
actually
does

Core function

Multiple Independent Timers

  • Run as many stopwatches as you need simultaneously
  • Copy a running timer to start timing someone else mid-race
  • Each timer keeps its own splits and history
  • Name your timers so you know which is which
  • Group related timers together for easy management
organization

Organization That Makes Sense

  • Name your timers with runner names, event types, or whatever makes sense for your situation
  • Group related timers together by event, team, session, or any way that helps you stay organized
  • Archive old sessions to keep current ones clean and focused
  • Search through your timing history when you need to reference past data
Timing

Timing Features You'll Actually Use

  • Record splits during races without stopping the main timer
  • Add lap markers for track events to keep track of distance and pacing
  • Note events and measurements as they happen
  • Add timestamps for specific moments you want to remember
  • Review timing patterns and trends from your sessions
Advanced

Advanced Group Management

  • Set up timing groups for different scenarios - parallel starts for races, sequential handoffs for relays, or independent timing for general use
  • The app understands different timing contexts and provides relevant tools for each
  • Bulk operations for starting, stopping, or managing multiple timers at once

Built for
real conditions

cloud_off Works offline

Your data lives on your device, not in the cloud. Whether there's no internet, or no cell service, it will continue to work just as it always does.

 battery_charging_full Battery Conscious

Designed to not drain your phone during long meets or training sessions. The app has been used for 5+ hour events without noticeable battery impact beyond normal usage.

 verified Actually reliable

Your timing data isn't tied to keeping a browser window open or staying in the app. The underlying timing mechanism ensures accuracy regardless of what else you do with your device.

 devices Responsive interface

Works on phones, tablets, laptops. Touch or mouse. Portrait or landscape. The interface adapts to your device and how you're using it.

 security Privacy by design

No accounts required, no personal data collection. Everything happens on your device. Your timing data never leaves your browser. I track feature usage to see what's getting used versus what is ignored, but that's all anonymous.
